Daydreamin'
Written in 2019

Called an "esoteric rejection of modern society," Daydreamin' cuts at the core of the isolation inside of the pandemic-era lockdowns. Sean McDeary, colonel overseeing an interstellar customs port, reacts adversely to a concentration substance that is pedelled by his work. After an underwhelming visit with his doctor, who turned the problem to Sean secretly having a coffee, Sean makes his decision to leave this behind him.
