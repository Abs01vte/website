Do You?

Could I ease your mind

just like you do mine?

Could I be your one

where others are none? 

Could I see the way you see yourself

from behind the curtains of your eyes?

Could you teach me everything there is to know

about the way that you feel when I see you?

Could you tell me?

Would it be hard?

Would it be too much to bear?

Would you crumble under the pressure?

Or would you tell me 

the truth that hurts?


Could I go swimming

in the depths of your serenity?

Could I go plunging head-first

into the shallows of your irises?

Or would that be the reason that I

can no longer be without you?

If I raised my hands and stopped all time

would it be me you spent eternity with?

If I leapt before the train of unity

would you be the conductor?

If my heart were to stop beating

would you know that you're the reason

that it started beating in the first place?


If the stars were to fall from the sky

and crash onto our bed of hopes and dreams

would you count them like sheep

until I relaxed into your arms?

If I were to one day wake no more

would that be the day you moved on?

Or would that be the day that you held on

to my ethereal form as you did to my corporeal form?


My answer is as easy as being asked 

What color is the sky?

What color is the grass?

Who is the one you love?

Because my answer is always you

Because you are the question and the answer

Because you are you

Because I'd rather have no one else


And I don't even know 

If you feel the same 

Or if you just mirror

Like I do to you

When I'm transfixed

By your beauty

By your majesty

By the solemn oath

I swore to myself
