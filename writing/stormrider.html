Stormrider

Indigo clouds delineate the boundary Between what is bright and what is dark And though the light will soon fade To the overcoming presence of This umber mass riding in I feel no ounce of fear No drop of anxiety Merely the desire to go forth Beat back its driving rains With my bare body Fight back against its winds With just my legs on the ground For those same ill-fated breezes That drove it to be over me Will themselves relocate this storm And bathe me, once again, In those dulcet rays of sunlight
