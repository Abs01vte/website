Death of Civil

Light trickles through the curtains Approaching the spot upon which I lay Tracing lines to me and away again Without a single motion's energy I pay Live, die, kill, survive Fight, win, steal, take All these the morality around And all these sins to my ears Monopolize, jeopardize Accumulate as much wealth as possible Detract, destroy Extract as much human essence as possible All for the gain of the one who is atop All for the glory of the eternal rulers All for the detriment of the lowers All for the pecuniary mining of all And with all this echoing into my room I still stay where I was when it all began For it all shall come again and soon And thus, why should I care and do what I can?
