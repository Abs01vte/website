Musashi

A short breath of relief A strong conviction of will A single look And a single action One strike Blade raised overhead Slashing down towards their head Time slows to a crawl The enemy blade gets so close That the swordsman leans back slightly With his headband falling to the sand In two pieces But not without revenge For the strike lead by the swordsman Clears clean through the enemy's forehead And right down to his chin Cleaved into two, the head splits Without agency any longer The body slumps to the ground Victorious swordsman flourishes and sheaths His blade
